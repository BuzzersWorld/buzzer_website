Community Guidelines

Last updated: February 22, 2022

Buzzer's mission is to promote and reward creativity. We're building a platform for people to connect with each other. Our Community Guidelines guide a way to do this safely for you and all other members on the Buzzer. We value all members' physical and personal safety. Buzzer encourages and protects all users to be their true selves. We will remove any content including video, audio, images, comments, captions, and other text that violates our Community Guidelines.


The following will be violations of our guidelines:

  • Anything that promotes or encites violence
  • Any violent and/or graphic content
  • Supporting hate, terrorist, and/or dangerous organizations
  • Content that includes firearms, alcohol, drugs, other controlled substances, gambling
  • Violations of the law or criminal activity and the promotion of either
  • Frauds or scams
  • Leaking private or personal data or information
  • Any type of harassment or bullying
  • Attacks on race, culture, religion, sex, gender, disability, and other human characteristics
  • Self-harm, suicide, and other violent or dangerous acts. We are in the best interest of all users' personal and mental health. If you or someone you know struggle with thoughts of self-harm or suicide, please contact local emergency services or the suicide prevention hotline here.
  • Adult nudity and sexual activity
  • Content that is not yours. We value integrity, especially as we declare winners for contests. Buzzer can remove content and winners should there be any impersonation or copyright violations.
